Financial Donation

Please join our charitable partners by making a financial gift. You can provide general support or designate your gift to a specific area. Your financial gift can help save thousands of lives struggling from illnesses. Your gift can provide the necessary medicine, treatment, and other essentials. Financial donations can be made online, by phone, or mail.

How to Give

Monthly Giving

Monthly giving ensures our organization has a steady stream of funds to help more patients in need. For as little as $5 a month, you can help us save more lives each year.

Support for Urgent Cases

We provide fast, competent support for urgent cases. We organize direct on-site support when necessary. Your small contribution can make a huge difference to those in need.

Host a Blood Drive

Inspiring others to give blood by hosting a blood drive is a rewarding way to benefit your community. Are you a high school or college student? We can help you organize the blood drive at a location of your choice and earn up to $500 scholarship for your help at the Blood Donation Center.

We provide pre-drive support, including posters/signage, educational materials, etc. On the day of the blood drive, we provide staff, donor beds, refreshments, and you provide the donors.
